Urban Vision Hackathon: The AI Challenge

About AI Urban Mobility Initiative

The AI Mobility Challenge is a joint effort by IISc, Airawat Research Foundation (IIT-K), and the Bangalore Traffic Police to create one of the largest annotated traffic image datasets in India. Indian traffic conditions are unique, with mixed vehicle types, varying road rules, and dense congestion. Accurate AI models trained on such real-world data can drive impactful mobility solutions, including traffic optimization, smart signal planning, and congestion forecasting.

Why the Image Annotation Hackathon?

To build this dataset, we are organizing a nationwide image annotation hackathon, where students will help classify and annotate traffic images from Bangalore's safety cameras. This will improve computer vision models for better urban mobility solutions. Participants will progress through a gamified challenge, refining AI training data at each stage.

Who Can Participate?

Undergraduate students from any discipline
Teams of 2-4 members from the same college
