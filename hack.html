About AI Urban Mobility Initiative

The AI Mobility Challenge is a collaboration between IISc, Airawat Research Foundation, and the Bangalore Traffic Police, aiming to build India's largest traffic image dataset. The goal is to enhance AI models for urban mobility, including traffic optimization, congestion forecasting, and smart signal planning.
